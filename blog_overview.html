Data Science Blog

Using established techniques to understand data

July 2, 2024 Blake U.

In this blog, I will discuss the data science concepts I've been learning throughout my undergraduate program at Iowa State University. For those familiar with data science, you'll know there are two main types of learning models used in the field: supervised and unsupervised learning. For the purposes of this blog, I will focus on supervised learning, which is more suitable for this blog because it provides clear parameters for model development.

I have broken my blog into multiple sections, such as statistical concepts, linear regression, KNN (k-nearest neighbors), and more. Each section covers a topic or technique related to statistical learning.Within each section, I will have blog posts that provide examples of the topic being used or discussed. The structure of the blog will be:

  1. Initial question that a project lead or client may have.
  2. I'll take the relevant data for the question and use a statistical model to analyze the data.
  3. I will provide advice and insights in a simple and concise manner.
